Saludos is a bar and kitchen on Av. Campeche in Puerto Peñasco. We opened in 2024 and we're not trying to be anything fancy. Good cocktails, real food, cold beer, a pool table, and bands on the Backyard Stage on the weekends. That's it.

If you're in Rocky Point and want somewhere to actually hang out, this is the place.

Saludos.

The Good Stuff

Live Music on the Backyard Stage

The stage. The band. The night air. We bring in live acts regularly, and there's nothing else quite like it in Rocky Point. Follow us on social so you never miss a show.

Real Food. Real Kitchen.

This is Bar AND Cocina. Burgers, chicken caesar salad, jalapeño poppers, onion rings, fish sandwiches. Real food, not just bar snacks. Eat before you drink. Or during. We don't judge.

Cold Drinks. Full Bar.

Dos Equis on draft, cocktails with tajin rims, full spirits shelf behind the bar. Order another round. You're on vacation.

The Bar

A real bar. Cold drinks. Good vibes.

Murals on the walls, pendant lights, a full spirits shelf, and a pool table in the corner. Saludos has the kind of interior that makes you want to stay for one more round.

The back bar is stocked. The bartenders know what they're doing. Whether you want a tajin margarita or a cold draft, we've got you covered.
