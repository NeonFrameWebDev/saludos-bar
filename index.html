Saludos Bar & Cocina sits right on El Mirador, Rocky Point's original beachfront strip. We opened in 2024 and we keep it simple: cold drinks, a full kitchen, a pool table, and live bands on the Backyard Stage most weekends.

Happy hour runs until 7 PM every day. Wednesday we do a smash burger for 100 pesos. Friday it's tacos for 20. Come in hungry.

The Good Stuff

Live Music on the Backyard Stage

Real bands, open sky, sand under your feet. We bring in live acts most weekends. There's nothing else quite like it on the Mirador. Follow us to catch the next show.
